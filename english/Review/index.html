PRTG The Network Monitoring Tool

I would like to discuss the thing that calls itself PRTG, PRTG is a network monitoring system. It’s a relativily cheap piece of software, of course there are open-source alternatives which are free nonetheless. I’m writing about this subject because I worked excessively with it at my training company, I must say that the software is good. I wouldn’t recommend it though, because I don’t think it’s priceworthy.

I would like to start off with the pricing, if you have a network with under 100 sensors, It’s free! But believe me you easily go beyond 400 sensors and that’s just a small network. I tried it on the network at my training company and got over 2,000 sensors. Luckily the 15-day trial has unlimited sensors, if you need 500 sensors it costs you €1,452.00. However most people will need unlimited sensors which costs €12.100,00. So it’s really expensive because Zabbix, the open-source alternative, offers unlimited sensors for free! But my training company didn’t believe open-sourcing was positive! This resulted in them spending way too much money for a network monitor, at least in my opinion.

As for the user interface, I have to say it’s really user-friendly. Everything is clear and direct, I easily found my way around the software. With some research it was easy to get the hang of it, sometimes it was not completely clear. This also came out of a lot of comments that are posted on their website. Finding reviews for this piece of software was not that easy because there are no big reviews, only comments and small texts. Out of all these small texts, I made a simple conclusion.

PRTG is an easy, userfriendly software that monitors your network with ease. Unfortunately, it’s expensive.
